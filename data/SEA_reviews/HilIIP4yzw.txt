 **Summary:**
The paper discusses the implementation of a flipped classroom approach in two master's courses at the Lebanese University. It details the steps taken using the ADDIE model, including the development of instructional materials and the use of various tools such as Padlet, Quiz-Maker, and Answergarden. The paper provides an overview of the challenges and benefits of the flipped classroom, highlighting the need for student motivation and the role of technology in facilitating learning. Despite its focus on a practical implementation, the paper lacks original research, relying heavily on existing literature and anecdotal evidence, which limits its contribution to the field.

**Strengths:**
- The paper provides a detailed description of the steps taken to implement a flipped classroom approach, including the use of the ADDIE model and various tools such as Padlet, Quiz-Maker, and Answergarden.
- The authors have highlighted the challenges and benefits of the flipped classroom, which is crucial for educators considering this approach.
- The paper is well-structured and easy to follow, making it accessible to a broad audience.
- The authors have provided an overview of the flipped classroom approach and its effectiveness, which is beneficial for educators seeking to understand this teaching method.

**Weaknesses:**
- The paper lacks original research and primarily relies on existing literature and anecdotal evidence, which limits its contribution to the field.
- There is a notable absence of quantitative data to support the claims made about the effectiveness of the flipped classroom approach, which undermines the paper's credibility.
- The paper does not adequately address the challenges specific to the implementation at the Lebanese University, which could have provided valuable insights for other institutions.
- The paper does not discuss the limitations of the flipped classroom approach, which is crucial for a balanced view of the method.
- The writing quality needs improvement, with numerous spelling and grammatical errors that detract from the professionalism of the paper.
- The paper does not include a detailed discussion on the challenges faced during the implementation of the flipped classroom, which could have provided valuable insights for other institutions.

**Questions:**
