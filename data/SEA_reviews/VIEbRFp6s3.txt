The paper introduces the OG-MARL dataset, a significant contribution to the offline multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL) community. This dataset, consisting of diverse environments such as SMAC, MAMuJoCo, PettingZoo, and others, provides a rich set of datasets for training and evaluating MARL algorithms.
