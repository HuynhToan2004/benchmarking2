 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel approach to optimizing the overapproximation of ReLU activations in neural networks using polynomial relaxations. The authors propose a method to optimize the parameters of these relaxations to achieve tighter bounds, enhancing the robustness verification of neural networks. The approach involves the use of polynomial relaxations for ReLU activations and a gradient-based optimization technique to optimize the parameters, aiming to provide tighter bounds compared to existing methods. The paper presents experimental results that demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method, albeit with some concerns regarding the practicality and scalability of the approach.
