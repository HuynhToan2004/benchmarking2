 **Summary:**
The paper introduces DipDNN, a novel invertible neural network architecture designed to address the computational challenges associated with traditional invertible DNNs. DipDNN employs a decomposed invertible pathway that decomposes the nested design into simpler, more computationally efficient layers. This design allows for the enforcement of one-to-one mapping in each layer, ensuring strict invertibility without compromising the universal approximation capability. The methodology is supported by theoretical proofs and empirical evidence, showing that DipDNN can effectively recover input data and can be integrated into parallel structures for enhanced bi-directional learning accuracy. The paper also explores the integration of physics embedding and regularization to improve generalization and stability.

**Strengths:**
- The paper introduces a novel architecture, DipDNN, which is a significant contribution to the field of invertible neural networks.
- The theoretical proofs provided are solid, including the universal approximation property of DipDNN, which is a notable strength.
- The paper is well-structured, clearly written, and includes a comprehensive review of related works, which helps contextualize the research.
- The empirical results are convincing, showing the effectiveness of DipDNN in various applications such as image restoration and state estimation.
- The use of regularization techniques and the integration of physics embedding are innovative approaches that enhance the robustness and generalization capabilities of DipDNN.

**Weaknesses:**
- The paper lacks a clear comparison with existing methods, particularly in terms of computational efficiency and accuracy.
- The motivation behind the research and the practical applications of DipDNN are not clearly articulated, which could limit the understanding and relevance of the work.
- The experimental results, while promising, are not sufficiently detailed or comprehensive to fully demonstrate the advantages of DipDNN over existing methods.
- The paper could benefit from a more detailed discussion on the limitations and potential drawbacks of DipDNN, as well as a broader exploration of its applications.
- There are minor issues with clarity and presentation, such as unclear figures and inconsistencies in the notation and terminology used.

**Questions:**
- Can the authors provide a clearer comparison of DipDNN with existing methods, including a discussion on the computational efficiency and accuracy of DipDNN?
- How does DipDNN compare in terms of computational cost and performance with other invertible DNN architectures, particularly in applications where speed is critical?
- Could the authors elaborate on the practical applications of DipDNN and provide real-world examples or case studies that demonstrate its effectiveness?
- How does the regularization technique used in DipDNN impact the performance and stability of the network, especially in complex or noisy data environments?
- Is there a possibility of extending the use of DipDNN in generative modeling, and how might this affect the network's capabilities and performance?
- Could the authors clarify the notation and terminology used in the paper to improve readability and understanding?
