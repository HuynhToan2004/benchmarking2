 **Summary:**
The paper addresses the black-box optimization problem of diffusion models, specifically targeting the fine-tuning of the inference phase to enhance the generation of user-preferred content. It formulates this problem as a sequential black-box optimization and introduces a stochastic adaptive sequential optimization algorithm (SABSO) to optimize cumulative black-box scores. Theoretical analysis supports the convergence of this algorithm, with empirical results demonstrating its effectiveness in generating target-guided images with high target scores. The paper also provides a comprehensive convergence analysis and empirical results, although it lacks detailed discussions on the convergence of the proposed algorithm and the practical implications of its application in real-world scenarios.
