 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel approach to solving the Schrödinger Bridge Problem (SBP) using stochastic variational minimization principles. It proposes a new stochastic Lagrangian that integrates physical constraints such as Fisher information and cross-entropy to construct policies predicting drift velocities. The paper demonstrates the utility of this approach through experiments on toy models, showing that the entropy gap between forward and backward trajectories can be minimized, potentially improving the convergence of SBP solutions. Despite its innovative approach, the paper suffers from significant presentation issues, including a lack of clarity in the experimental setup and insufficient justification for the experimental results.
