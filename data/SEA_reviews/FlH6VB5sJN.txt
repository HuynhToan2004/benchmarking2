 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel multi-compartment spiking neural network (SNN) model named Parallel Multi-compartment Spiking Neuron (PMSN), designed to handle complex temporal dynamics in sensory signals. This model is inspired by biological neurons and is capable of capturing intricate interactions among neuronal compartments, preserving and integrating multi-scale temporal information for effective sequential modeling. The PMSN model is optimized for parallel training on GPU-accelerated machine learning frameworks, enhancing training efficiency. The paper demonstrates the model's effectiveness through extensive experiments, showcasing improved accuracy and simulation speed, along with favorable trade-offs between computational cost and accuracy.
