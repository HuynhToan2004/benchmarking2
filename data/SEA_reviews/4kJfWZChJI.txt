 **Summary:**
The paper introduces the Spectral Meta Estimation and Ensemble (SMEE) approach for domain generalization (DG), focusing on the use of domain-specific expert models and unsupervised ensemble learning to construct a weighted ensemble for test samples. The SMEE methodology employs meta performance estimation and spectral unsupervised ensemble methods to enhance model selection and transferability estimation, respectively. The approach is tested on the DomainBed benchmark, demonstrating superior performance compared to state-of-the-art methods. However, the paper lacks a detailed theoretical analysis of the proposed method and does not include a comprehensive discussion on the limitations and potential negative societal impacts.
