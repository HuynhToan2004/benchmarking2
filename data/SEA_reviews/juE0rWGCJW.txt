 **Summary:**
The paper introduces ETGraph, a novel dataset that integrates Ethereum and Twitter data, aiming to enhance blockchain analysis by incorporating social network data. The dataset comprises 2 million nodes and 30 million edges from Ethereum transactions and 1 million nodes and 3 million edges from Twitter following data, linked by 30,667 Ethereum addresses and verified Twitter accounts from OpenSea. The paper explores the utility of this dataset in various tasks such as link prediction, wash-trading detection, and matching Ethereum addresses to Twitter accounts. The experimental results demonstrate that Twitter data significantly enhances Ethereum analysis, providing a valuable resource for future research in blockchain and graph learning.
