 **Summary:**
The paper investigates the prediction and estimation risks of the ridge-less least squares estimator under a variety of regression error assumptions, including non-iid and non-spherical errors. It presents finite-sample expressions for these risks, highlighting the impact of overparameterization on reducing risks. The paper also discusses the implications of these findings in scenarios where the feature matrix is left-spherical, which allows for the derivation of exact expressions for risk components. The theoretical contributions are supported by numerical simulations, although the paper's novelty and the practical implications of the results are questioned.

- The authors provide a detailed analysis of the prediction and estimation risks of the ridge-less least squares estimator, which is a significant contribution to the field.
