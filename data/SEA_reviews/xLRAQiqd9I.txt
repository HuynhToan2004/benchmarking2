 **Summary:**
The paper introduces GeoMFormer, a novel Transformer-based architecture for molecular modeling that separates invariant and equivariant representations through cross-attention mechanisms. The model is designed to learn both invariant and equivariant features, which are crucial for molecular modeling. It is evaluated across various datasets, including OC20, PCQM4Mv2, Molecule3D, and N-Body Simulation, showing strong performance on both invariant and equivariant tasks. The architecture is flexible and general, allowing it to be viewed as a special case of existing methods, and it achieves state-of-the-art results on several benchmarks.
