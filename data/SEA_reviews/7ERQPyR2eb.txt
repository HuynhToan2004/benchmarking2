 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel method for generating realistic talking portrait videos from a single image, leveraging a large image-to-plane model that integrates 3D prior knowledge from a 3D face generative model. This model is used to reconstruct 3D representations of the input image, which are then animated using a motion adapter and rendered with a head-torso-background super-resolution model. The method supports both video and audio inputs, allowing for realistic and natural talking portrait videos. The approach includes a pre-trained large Image-to-Plane model, a motion adapter, a head-torso-background super-resolution model, and a generic audio-to-motion model, which are evaluated against several baselines and shown to outperform them in terms of identity preservation, visual quality, and audio-lip synchronization.
