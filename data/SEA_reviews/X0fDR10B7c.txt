 **Summary:**
The paper explores the integration of predictive coding with causal inference, demonstrating how predictive coding can be adapted to perform interventional and counterfactual inference in known and unknown causal graphs. This approach leverages the ability of predictive coding to model interventions and perform structure learning from observational data. The paper presents a method that adjusts the variational free energy objective to perform interventional and counterfactual queries, showing promising results in both synthetic and real-world datasets. The proposed method extends the capabilities of predictive coding beyond correlation analysis, allowing for the modeling of causal relationships.
