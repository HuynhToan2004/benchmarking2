 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel approach to video synthesis by focusing on the disentanglement of appearance and motion. It leverages a flow-based image animator and a latent motion diffusion model to represent motion as a sequence of flow maps, enhancing the coherence of synthesized videos. The methodology involves training a latent motion diffusion model on the motion code from a pre-trained image animator, which is then used to generate videos. The approach is evaluated against several benchmarks and shows improved results in terms of video quality and coherence. However, the paper has been criticized for its incremental nature, lack of detailed comparisons with existing methods, and potential issues with the clarity and depth of its technical contributions.

**Strengths:**
- The paper presents a novel approach to video synthesis by focusing on the disentanglement of appearance and motion, which is a significant challenge in the field.
- The methodology is well-articulated, with clear explanations of the technical contributions and the experimental setup.
- The paper includes comprehensive evaluations and comparisons with state-of-the-art methods, demonstrating the effectiveness of the proposed approach.
- The results are visually appealing, showing significant improvements in video quality and coherence compared to existing methods.
- The paper is well-written, making it accessible and easy to follow, which is crucial for a broad audience.

**Weaknesses:**
- The paper lacks detailed comparisons with existing methods, particularly in terms of the specific advantages and disadvantages of the proposed method over existing approaches.
- The technical contributions are somewhat incremental, as the main idea of using a latent motion diffusion model seems similar to previous methods like TATS.
- The paper does not provide sufficient details on the training and inference processes, which could hinder reproducibility and understanding of the methodology.
- There are some issues with the clarity and depth of the technical descriptions, particularly in the explanation of how the latent motion diffusion model operates and its integration with other components.
- The paper could benefit from more detailed discussions on the limitations and potential negative societal impacts of the proposed method.
