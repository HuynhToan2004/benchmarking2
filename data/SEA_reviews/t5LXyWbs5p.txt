 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel frequency-aware masked autoencoder (bioFAME) designed for pretraining on multimodal biosignals, specifically focusing on the frequency domain to address distributional shifts in biosignals. The bioFAME model incorporates a frequency-aware transformer and a frequency-maintain pretraining strategy, which are evaluated on various transfer experiments on unimodal time series, demonstrating an average improvement of 5.5% in classification accuracy over the previous state-of-the-art. The model is designed to be robust against distributional shifts and is adaptable to diverse tasks and modalities, showing effectiveness in modality mismatch scenarios.
