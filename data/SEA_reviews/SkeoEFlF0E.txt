 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel neural tensor model, Neural Additive Tensor Decomposition (NeAT), which aims to address the challenges of modeling non-linear structures in sparse tensors. NeAT leverages neural networks to learn non-linear interactions within each component separately, thereby maintaining interpretability and preserving the properties of classical linear models like CPD. The model is evaluated against various baselines and shows superior performance in sparse tensor completion tasks, particularly in link prediction. Despite its innovative approach, concerns are raised about the clarity of the motivation behind the model's design and the lack of comprehensive comparisons with existing methods.
