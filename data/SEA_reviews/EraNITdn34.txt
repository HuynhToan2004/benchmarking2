 **Summary:**
The paper introduces TabToken, a novel method for enhancing the transferability of feature tokens in tabular data, focusing on the pre-training and fine-tuning stages. TabToken employs a contrastive regularization objective to align feature tokens with their semantic meanings, improving the transferability of tokens between different tasks. This approach is validated through extensive experiments on various datasets, showing that TabToken outperforms existing methods in transfer learning settings. The paper also discusses the practical implications of TabToken in real-world applications, such as medical diagnoses, where it can potentially improve diagnostic accuracy by leveraging shared features across datasets.

- The paper is well-written, clear, and easy to follow, with a clear motivation and a focus on the transferability of feature tokens in tabular data.
- The proposed method, TabToken, is novel and effective, demonstrated through experiments that show TabToken outperforms other methods in transfer learning settings.
- The methodology is simple and effective, with strong experimental results that support the claims made.
