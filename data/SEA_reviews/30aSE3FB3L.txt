 **Summary:**
The paper explores the extension of deep neural networks (DNNs) from Euclidean to Riemannian manifolds, specifically focusing on Symmetric Positive Definite (SPD) and Grassmann manifolds. The authors propose new architectures for fully-connected and convolutional layers, multinomial logistic regression, and graph convolutional networks on these manifolds. The methodology is supported by theoretical insights and validated through experiments on various datasets, demonstrating the effectiveness of the proposed methods in tasks like human action recognition and node classification. The paper aims to generalize the gyrovector space approach to these manifolds, providing a novel perspective on neural network design and training.
