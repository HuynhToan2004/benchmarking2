 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel Meta-Black-Box Optimization (MetaBBO) framework named SYMBOL, which utilizes symbolic equation learning to generate closed-form optimization rules. This approach allows for the automated discovery of black-box optimizers, which is a significant departure from traditional methods that rely on predefined optimizers. SYMBOL employs a Symbolic Equation Generator (SEG) that generates closed-form optimization rules dynamically, and three training strategies based on reinforcement learning to meta-learn the SEG. The framework is evaluated against various benchmarks and shows promising results, with the potential to generalize well to unseen tasks. However, concerns were raised regarding the complexity of the training process, the generalization capabilities, and the fairness of the comparisons made with other methods.
