 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel method called Manifold Kernel Rank Reduced Regression (MKRRR), which extends the Kernel Rank Reduced Regression (KRRR) to Riemannian manifolds. This extension allows the model to leverage the intrinsic geometric structure of data, which is particularly useful for complex, high-dimensional datasets. The method is tested on a 3D mandibular reconstruction task, demonstrating its effectiveness in handling high-level noise and its ability to reduce dimensionality while maintaining accuracy. The paper also discusses the robustness of the MKRRR model and provides a theoretical analysis of its convergence.
