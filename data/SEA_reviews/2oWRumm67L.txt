 **Summary:**
The paper introduces Light-MILPopt, a novel framework designed to solve large-scale Mixed Integer Linear Programs (MILPs) using a lightweight optimizer and a small training dataset. This framework is divided into four stages: Problem Formulation, Model-based Initial Solution Prediction, Problem Reduction, and Data-driven Optimization. The authors claim that their method outperforms existing ML-based optimization frameworks and large-scale solvers, demonstrating significant advantages in solving large-scale MILPs with small-scale optimizers and small training datasets. However, the paper's claims of novelty and the effectiveness of the proposed method are questioned, as it heavily relies on existing techniques and does not sufficiently distinguish itself from existing methods.
