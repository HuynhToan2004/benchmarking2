 **Summary:**
The paper investigates the conditions under which autoencoders can learn object-centric representations that generalize compositionally, focusing on the slot identifiability and compositional consistency of decoders. It introduces a compositional consistency regularizer to ensure the encoder can invert the decoder on novel combinations of latent slots, thus enabling the model to generalize out-of-distribution. The theoretical framework is supported by empirical results on synthetic image data, demonstrating the practical relevance of the assumptions. The paper also discusses the implementation of additivity in practice and its implications for the generalization of object-centric models.

**Strengths:**
- The paper provides a novel theoretical analysis of compositional generalization for object-centric learning, which is a significant contribution to the field.
- The authors present a clear and well-structured paper, with well-written theorems and definitions that are easy to understand.
- The paper includes a detailed theoretical framework and a compositional consistency regularizer, which are supported by empirical results demonstrating their effectiveness.
- The paper is well-motivated and addresses a critical gap in the understanding of compositional generalization in object-centric learning.
- The authors have provided a detailed explanation of the theoretical results and their practical implications, which could be useful for future research in the field.

**Weaknesses:**
- The paper is limited to object-centric learning, which may not be generalizable to other domains, such as language or music.
- The experimental results are limited to simple synthetic datasets, and the paper lacks real-world data experiments, which could enhance the practical applicability of the findings.
- The paper assumes a high degree of regularity in the latent space, which may not be realistic in more complex scenarios.
- The theoretical analysis relies on strong assumptions about the generator, which may not hold in practical scenarios.
- The paper does not provide a clear explanation of how the theoretical results could be applied in practical scenarios, especially in more complex or real-world settings.
- The paper does not discuss the limitations of its theoretical results, which could be a significant drawback for readers looking for a comprehensive understanding of the topic.

**Questions:**
