 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel method for determining the data domain of a target classifier, utilizing a generative model to refine the search process. The method employs a breadth-first search algorithm to iteratively refine the description of the data domain, guided by the classification outcomes of the target model. The approach is evaluated across various datasets, including CIFAR-10, Places365, and a binary image classifier for pneumonia detection, demonstrating its ability to identify specific attributes within the data domain. However, the method's reliance on a generative model raises concerns about its generalizability and the accuracy of the results.
