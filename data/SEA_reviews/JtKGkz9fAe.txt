 **Summary:**
The paper introduces ReFusion, a novel retrieval representation fusion framework aimed at enhancing the performance of non-knowledge-intensive (NKI) tasks in language models. ReFusion integrates a retrieval module that directly fuses retrieval representations into the language model, employing a bilevel optimization approach to optimize the fusion structure. This method is claimed to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of retrieval-augmented techniques, particularly in tasks where the input text is short and the retrieval results need to be integrated into the model's hidden states. The paper presents a comprehensive evaluation across 15 NKI tasks, demonstrating that ReFusion outperforms existing methods in most cases, although the performance gains are sometimes marginal.
