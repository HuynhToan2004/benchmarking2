 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel data augmentation-guided decoupled knowledge distillation (DADKD) framework for enhancing low-resolution fine-grained image classification. This method employs a data augmentation technique that generates hybrid images from high-resolution images, utilizing a channel attention mechanism and SnapMix. The knowledge distillation component of DADKD minimizes the differences between high-resolution and low-resolution image features. The framework was evaluated on three fine-grained datasets, showing promising results compared to state-of-the-art methods. However, the novelty of the method is questioned, as it seems to be a combination of existing techniques without significant innovation. The paper's writing and presentation also need improvement to enhance clarity and professionalism.

**Strengths:**
- The proposed method is intuitive and easy to follow, with a clear structure and well-written presentation.
- The paper is well-organized, making it easy for readers to understand the content and the contributions of the proposed method.
- The method achieves good results on three fine-grained datasets, demonstrating its effectiveness in practical applications.
- The use of a data augmentation-guided decoupled knowledge distillation framework is innovative, combining channel attention and SnapMix to generate hybrid images, which enhances the accuracy of classification recognition for low-resolution fine-grained images.
- The proposed method is simple and effective, with significant improvements over previous state-of-the-art methods.

**Weaknesses:**
- The novelty of the method is limited as it appears to be a combination of existing techniques without significant innovation.
- The paper lacks a comprehensive analysis of the experimental results, such as a detailed comparison with other methods and a thorough discussion of the results.
- The writing quality needs improvement, with numerous grammatical errors and unclear explanations that hinder understanding.
- The paper does not adequately discuss the limitations of the proposed method, which is crucial for understanding its applicability and potential drawbacks.
- There is a lack of comparison with other data augmentation and knowledge distillation methods, which could have provided a more robust evaluation of the proposed method.
- The paper's presentation could be improved by including more visualizations and better figures to illustrate the method's effectiveness.

**Questions:**
- How does the proposed method compare to other data augmentation and knowledge distillation methods, especially those that have been proposed in recent years?
- Can the authors provide more detailed explanations for the experimental results, particularly the significant improvements observed in the results?
- How does the proposed method perform on other low-resolution fine-grained image datasets, and what are the potential limitations or challenges in applying the method to these datasets?
- Could the authors clarify the role and impact of the channel focus mechanism in the data augmentation module?
- How does the proposed method handle the issue of noisy labels in low-resolution fine-grained image classification?
- Is there a possibility to extend the proposed method to other types of image classification tasks, such as scene recognition or object detection?
