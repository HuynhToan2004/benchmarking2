 **Summary:**
The paper explores the convergence of the two-timescale extragradient method to local minimax points in minimax problems. It extends the analysis of the two-timescale gradient descent ascent method to include cases where the Hessian with respect to the maximization variable is not nondegenerate. The authors introduce a new notion of restricted Schur complement and use dynamical systems theory to analyze the convergence behavior. They also provide a second-order characterization of local minimax points and discuss the relationship between two-timescale gradient descent and extragradient methods. The paper is well-written, clear, and extends the understanding of minimax optimization, although it relies heavily on existing theoretical frameworks and does not offer significant novelty in its proof techniques or methodological contributions.
