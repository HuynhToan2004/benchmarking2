 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel label differential privacy (LabelDP) algorithm named LabelDP-Pro, which integrates gradient projection with private stochastic gradient descent (DP-SGD) to enhance privacy and utility in machine learning models. The approach leverages the power of differential privacy (DP) to protect labels while preserving utility, particularly in high-privacy regimes. Theoretical analyses and empirical evaluations across various datasets demonstrate the algorithm's effectiveness in improving utility and maintaining privacy. The paper also discusses the practical implications of the proposed method, including its application in real-world scenarios like online advertising.

**Strengths:**
- The paper introduces a novel approach to label differential privacy (LabelDP) by integrating gradient projection with private stochastic gradient descent (DP-SGD), which is a significant advancement in the field.
- The methodology is well-explained, making it easy to understand and implement, and the paper provides a clear comparison with existing methods, highlighting the advantages of the proposed approach.
- The paper includes comprehensive theoretical analysis and empirical evaluations, which demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method in enhancing utility while maintaining privacy.
- The paper addresses a practical problem of label privacy, which is of significant importance in the real world, particularly in applications like online advertising where user-level privacy is crucial.
- The paper is well-written, making it accessible and easy to follow, with a clear presentation of the results and a detailed discussion of the proposed method.

**Weaknesses:**
- The paper does not provide a detailed comparison of the computational complexity of the proposed method with existing methods, which could be crucial for understanding the practical implications of the approach.
- The paper does not discuss the limitations of the proposed method, which could help in understanding the scope and applicability of the approach.
- The paper does not provide a detailed discussion on the potential societal impacts of the proposed method, which is essential for understanding the broader implications of the work.
- The paper does not include a detailed discussion on the privacy accounting and the noise multiplier used, which could affect the reproducibility and practical implementation of the proposed method.
- The paper does not include a discussion on the potential negative societal impacts of the proposed method, which is crucial for understanding the broader implications of the work.

**Questions:**

- How does the proposed method perform in scenarios where the privacy budget is larger, and what are the implications for utility?
- Could the authors elaborate on the potential societal impacts of the proposed method, both positive and negative?
- How does the proposed method compare in terms of privacy guarantees and utility to other state-of-the-art methods in the field of label differential privacy?
- Could the authors provide a detailed discussion on the privacy accounting and the noise multiplier used in the proposed method?
