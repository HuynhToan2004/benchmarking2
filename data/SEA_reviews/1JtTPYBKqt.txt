 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel problem called Neural Architecture Retrieval (NAR), which aims to retrieve similar neural architectures given a query architecture. The authors propose a two-stage graph embedding learning method that decomposes neural architectures into motifs and then reconstructs them, thereby reducing computational complexity. This method is supported by extensive experiments, including the creation of a dataset of 12,000 neural architectures, which is used to validate the effectiveness of the proposed approach. The paper is well-written and presents a clear motivation, though it lacks detailed comparisons with existing methods and a broader evaluation on different datasets and architectures.
