 **Summary:**
The paper explores the dynamics of Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD) in high-dimensional settings, specifically focusing on the alignment of SGD trajectories with outlier eigenspaces of the empirical Hessian and gradient matrices. The study is based on two canonical classification tasks: multi-class high-dimensional mixtures and either 1 or 2-layer neural networks. The authors demonstrate that SGD aligns with these outlier eigenspaces, which are crucial for understanding the training dynamics in high-dimensional spaces. The paper also provides theoretical insights into the spectral properties of the Hessian and gradient matrices, which are critical for the alignment of SGD trajectories.
