 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a method for continual test-time adaptation (CTA) that leverages exponential moving average (EMA) target prototypes and source prototypes to maintain and adapt the model to changing target domains. The proposed method involves updating target prototypes using reliable samples and aligning target features with source prototypes to minimize domain shifts. The approach is tested on datasets like CIFAR100-C and ImageNet-C, showing performance improvements over existing methods like EATA and TTAC. However, the method's novelty is questioned due to its similarity to existing methods, and the paper's experimental setup and comparisons are criticized for not being comprehensive or fair.
