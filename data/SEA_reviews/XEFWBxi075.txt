 **Summary:**
The paper introduces GRANDE, a novel gradient-based decision tree ensemble that utilizes a softsign split function and instance-wise weighting for learning hard, axis-aligned decision trees. GRANDE employs a dense representation of tree ensembles to facilitate backpropagation and jointly optimize all model parameters. It extends the GradTree approach by introducing a softsign split function and a weighting technique to enhance the learning of tree ensembles. The paper claims that GRANDE outperforms existing methods on most datasets, although the performance improvements are not consistently significant. The paper also discusses the limitations of the approach and suggests future directions for research.

- The method is well-motivated and the results are impressive, with a thorough experimental evaluation and a good comparison to state-of-the-art methods.
