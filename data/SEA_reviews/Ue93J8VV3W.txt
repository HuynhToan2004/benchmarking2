 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a new benchmark, TabGraphs, aimed at evaluating graph machine learning methods on tabular data with heterogeneous node features. It compares various models including GBDT, GNNs, and specialized models for graph-structured data, highlighting the potential of graph-structured data in improving predictive performance. The benchmark is diverse, encompassing various domains, prediction tasks, and graph structural properties. The paper provides insights into the effectiveness of different GNN architectures and numerical feature pre-processing strategies, emphasizing the need for tailored approaches for different datasets.

- The benchmark is diverse, covering various domains, prediction tasks, and graph structural properties, making it a valuable resource for researchers in both tabular and graph machine learning.
