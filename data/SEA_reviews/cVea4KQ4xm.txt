 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a novel approach to fairness in machine learning by focusing on the concept of equal treatment, which is distinct from the traditional demographic parity. It proposes a method using Shapley values to measure explanation distributions, which can reveal whether the model's treatment is independent of protected attributes. This method is tested on both synthetic and real-world datasets, demonstrating its effectiveness in detecting unfairness in model explanations. The paper also provides a theoretical framework and practical tools for assessing fairness, aiming to address the limitations of existing fairness metrics.

**Strengths:**
- The paper introduces a novel method for measuring fairness using Shapley values, which is both theoretically sound and practical.
- The method is well-explained, with clear theoretical and empirical evidence supporting the claims.
- The paper is well-written, making it accessible and easy to understand, with detailed examples that aid in comprehension.
- The concept of fairness is explored in depth, focusing on the interaction between features and protected attributes, which is a unique approach.
- The paper provides a comprehensive analysis of the relationship between demographic parity and equal treatment, and the theoretical results are well-explained.
- The availability of a Python package for the proposed method enhances the practical applicability of the research.

**Weaknesses:**
- The paper lacks a clear definition and explanation of the concept of "equal treatment," which is crucial for understanding the theoretical underpinnings of the method.
- The paper does not adequately address the limitations of the method, particularly concerning its applicability to real-world scenarios and the potential for bias in model explanations.
- The experimental setup and results are not convincingly presented, with some figures and results not clearly explaining the experimental design or outcomes.
- The paper could benefit from a more detailed discussion on the philosophical foundations of fairness and the practical implications of the proposed method in real-world scenarios.
- The paper does not sufficiently compare its method with existing fairness metrics, which could help in understanding the relative advantages and disadvantages of the proposed approach.

**Questions:**
