 **Summary:**
The paper explores the robustness of watermarking techniques for Large Language Models (LLMs) against various attacks such as paraphrasing and copy-pasting. It evaluates the effectiveness of watermarking in realistic settings and compares it to other detection methods like retrieval-based approaches. The study includes experiments with human paraphrasing and automated paraphrasing tools, demonstrating that watermarking can be effective even under adversarial conditions. However, the paper's main contribution is its empirical analysis, which shows that watermarking remains detectable even after modifications, suggesting its practical utility in real-world scenarios.

**Strengths:**
- The paper provides a comprehensive evaluation of the robustness of watermarking against various attacks, including human paraphrasing and automated paraphrasing tools.
- The empirical analysis is thorough, demonstrating the effectiveness of watermarking in real-world scenarios, even when the text is modified.
- The paper is well-written, easy to follow, and provides a clear motivation for the research, highlighting the importance of watermarking in detecting LLM-generated text.
- The study includes a human study, which adds valuable insights into the practical applicability of watermarking.
- The paper includes a detailed analysis of the impact of watermarking on the quality of generated text, which is an important consideration for practical applications.

**Weaknesses:**
- The paper lacks a discussion on the limitations of watermarking, which could help in understanding its practical applicability and potential shortcomings.
- The paper could benefit from a more detailed discussion on the societal implications of watermarking, especially in terms of privacy concerns and potential misuse.
- The experimental setup could be improved by including more diverse datasets and LLMs to enhance the generalizability of the findings.
- The paper does not provide a detailed analysis of the impact of watermarking on the quality of generated text, which is a crucial aspect for practical applications.
- The paper does not include a detailed discussion on the computational complexity of watermarking, which could be a significant concern for practical implementations.
- The paper does not provide a detailed analysis of the impact of watermarking on the performance of the LLM, which could be a significant concern for practical applications.
