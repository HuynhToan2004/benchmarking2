 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a streaming algorithm for estimating the outer (d-k)-radius of a set of points in Rd, which involves finding a k-dimensional flat that minimizes the maximum distance between the points and the flat. The algorithm is based on a coreset construction method that selects a subset of points, which can be used to approximate the optimal solution of the problem. The authors claim that their algorithm provides a poly(k, log n) approximation to the outer (d-k)-radius using O(d k^3 log^3 n) bits of space and O(nnz(A) log n + d poly(k, log n)) time. The paper also explores the application of this algorithm to other problems such as subspace approximation and width estimation.
