 **Summary:**
The paper introduces SMAFace, a novel face recognition method that integrates sample mining into margin-based methods to enhance the performance of face recognition algorithms. The method prioritizes information-dense samples, including hard and easy samples, to improve the robustness and adaptability of the model. SMAFace uses a probability-driven mining strategy to navigate hard samples, which is evaluated on four renowned datasets, showing superior performance compared to state-of-the-art methods. The paper is well-structured and includes a thorough mathematical analysis, visualizations, and empirical tests, demonstrating the effectiveness of the proposed method.

**Strengths:**

- The methodology is well-explained, making it easy to understand and replicate.
- The paper is well-organized, clearly written, and provides a detailed mathematical analysis.
- The proposed method achieves state-of-the-art (SOTA) performance on four renowned face recognition datasets, demonstrating its effectiveness.
