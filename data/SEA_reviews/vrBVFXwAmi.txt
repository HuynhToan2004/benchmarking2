 **Summary:**
The paper introduces Q-TAPE, a novel pre-trained model designed for estimating quantum properties using quantum data generated by quantum computers. Q-TAPE leverages a transformer-based architecture to learn the probability distribution of measurement outcomes, which is then fine-tuned for specific tasks such as quantum phase classification and correlation function prediction. The model is trained in an unsupervised manner using a transformer decoder, which processes quantum measurement outcomes and physical conditions to predict quantum properties. The model's effectiveness is demonstrated through experiments on two quantum models, showing that it outperforms existing methods in tasks like phase classification and correlation function prediction.
