 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a new dataset, MIMIC-R3G, designed for real-world radiology report generation, which incorporates various clinical interactions and contextual information. It proposes a new benchmark dataset and a unified data generation pipeline using ChatGPT to automatically generate context data. The paper also introduces a domain-enhanced multimodal model, DeMMo, which incorporates a medical domain vision encoder into a pretrained general domain multimodal model to enhance its domain-specific capabilities. The model is evaluated against existing interactive report generation frameworks and state-of-the-art encoder-decoder style report generation models, demonstrating competitive performance. However, concerns were raised about the lack of comprehensive evaluations, particularly in terms of factual correctness and the generalizability of the results.

**Strengths:**
- The paper introduces a new problem setting for real-world report generation, which emulates clinical practices by incorporating various clinical interactions and contextual information.
- It proposes a new benchmark dataset, MIMIC-R3G, where a unified framework is designed to automatically generate the requisite context data, leveraging the power of LLM.
- The paper develops DeMMo, a large multimodal model with domain-specific capability enhanced via incorporating a general domain Flamingo with an additional medical vision encoder.
- The paper is well-written, easy to follow, and provides a comprehensive evaluation of the proposed method, demonstrating competitive performance across all real-world tasks compared to existing interactive report generation frameworks and state-of-the-art encoder-decoder style report generation models.
- The data generation process is well-explained, and the paper provides a detailed analysis of the dataset, including its size, content, and diversity.
