 **Summary:**
The paper introduces a new dataset named LaDe, specifically designed for last-mile delivery research. This dataset is comprehensive, featuring detailed information on package, location, task-event, and courier data, collected over six months from various cities. It is the largest dataset of its kind, offering a significant resource for researchers in logistics, supply chain management, and spatio-temporal data mining. The dataset is benchmarked against three real-world tasks, demonstrating its utility and potential for further research. Despite its strengths, the paper has been criticized for its limited novelty and lack of detailed methodological descriptions, particularly in the data collection process and the benchmarking tasks.
